# Pedestrian avatar credits

The character FBX files in this folder were produced by `Tools/retarget_avatars.py`
from two CC0 (public domain) sources:

- **Meshes/textures:** "100 Avatars" rounds 1–2 by **Polygonal Mind**
  (https://github.com/madjin/100avatars), released under CC0 1.0 Universal.
- **Animations:** "Universal Animation Library" (standard/free edition) by
  **Quaternius** (https://quaternius.com), CC0 1.0 Universal; obtained from the
  glTF mirror at https://github.com/J-Ponzo/gltf-universal-animation-library.
  The clips Idle_Loop, Walk_Loop, Jog_Fwd_Loop, Idle_Talking_Loop and
  Walk_Formal_Loop were retargeted onto each avatar's Mixamo-style rig and
  baked into the FBX takes A_Idle, A_Walk, A_Jog, A_Talk, A_WalkFormal.

CC0 means no attribution is required, but both creators deserve it anyway.
